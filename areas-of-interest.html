Areas of Interest

Metabolic Disorders

The laboratory investigates diabetes mellitus with a focus on beta cell dysfunction and metabolic stress.

Non-coding RNA

We investigate microRNAs and other ncRNAs as biomarkers and therapeutic targets.

Calcium

Functional roles of calcium signaling across cardiovascular, metabolic, and neuromuscular systems.

Stem Cells

Human stem cell maturation and regenerative medicine strategies.

Translational Medicine

Bridging fundamental discoveries to clinical applications.

Aging and Healthy Longevity

Molecular mechanisms of aging, senescence, diet, and nutrition.

Clinical Research

Clinical research in cardiovascular medicine, diabetes, and aging.
